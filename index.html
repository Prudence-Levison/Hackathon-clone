Our Programs

ENGINEERING

We want our younger wowen toexplore different aspect of wha t it means to be an engineer in a fun hands in environment.Girls will learn leadership skills such as goal setting and efective communication and engage in activities that showcase different engineering activities. Participants will also design and create enginerring projects through outthe week under the guidance of our engineering Mentors and Institutions

TECHNOLOGY

The aim isto provide participants with the skills they needso they can be informed contributors and creators of digital world .We want to help shift the default to one where women have a strong equitable role in reading, writing and participating on the web- and ultimately,help lead the next wave of openess, innovation and opportunity online

We run STEM based projects for young girls aimed at equipping them with the requisite skill to solve problems around them.

SCIENCE

The aim is to arm young women who have a vested interest in science with the skills and confidence to pursue higher education and / or a career in STEM fields. At HackathonGirls, we recongnize that aceesing and particioating in STEM fields for women and girls is critical to achieving gender equality.

MATHEMATICS

Poor participation of girls in mathematics isparticularly troubling as this isa gateway discipline to other fields. It is not only advantageous to have studied intermediate por advanced level mathematics at secondary schools, but in many cases it is requisite to tertiary study in STEM discipline, such as engineering and computing and we hope to organiz programms to encourage more girls to dotheir maths well.
